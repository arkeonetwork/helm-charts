- Get the Arkeo Node IP by running these commands:

  export NODE_IP=$(kubectl get configmap {{ include "arkeo.fullname" . }}-external-ip -o jsonpath={.data.externalIP})
  echo $NODE_IP

- Visit RPC endpoint of the Arkeo Node:

  Mainnet:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:27147

  Testnet / Mocknet:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:26567
